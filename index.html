🔒 Spotify Queue Locker — Setup

This app keeps your Spotify playback locked to an album — no escaping allowed. To get started you need a free Spotify developer app Client ID.

Quick setup:

  • Go to developer.spotify.com/dashboard
  • Create an app (any name, any description)
  • Copy the Client ID and paste it below
  • In the app settings, add the Redirect URI shown below to the allowlist
  • Click Connect to Spotify — done!

Found in your Spotify Developer Dashboard under the app settings.
